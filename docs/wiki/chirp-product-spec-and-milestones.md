# Chirp Product Spec and Milestones

## Product Spec & Milestones

Chirp is a Nostr reference app with a product spec at docs/chirp/ covering milestones M0–M17 plus CX1–CX5 for post-v1 features. Chirp v1 inherits multi-account, negentropy, outbox, diagnostics, NIP-46, NIP-29, and follow/unfollow from the NMP framework's M0–M17 ladder. The genuine open frontier milestones are M10 (Blossom), M13 (WoT), M14 (UniFFI), and M17 (release). Local signer discovery (the Olas/Primal auto-link pattern) is a gap in the framework docs and is spec'd as CX3. V-68 tracks that iOS Chirp ships a hardcoded 21,000 msat zap default to production.
